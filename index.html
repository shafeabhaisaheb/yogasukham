Tabla

Tabla is taught by Satya Vaidyanathan, a disciple of Shri Muthu Kumar, who himself is a disciple of Ustad Alla Rakha of the Punjab gharana.

Tabla is a North Indian percussion instrument consisting of a set of two drums — a tonal drum tuned to a musical scale, and a bass drum. Tabla is a dynamic instrument with an extensive solo repertoire and also serves as the primary rhythmic accompaniment instrument for North Indian classical music. In the 20th century, tabla reached the masses worldwide as a result of the great work of masters like Ustad Alla Rakha and Pandit Kishan Maharaj. There are six main schools of tabla playing, named after the geographic regions in which they originated. Each school has its own unique style and approach to the instrument that differentiates it from the other schools.

Satya is a passionate teacher, focused on instilling the rigor needed to learn and practice this ancient art form while making it a fun and engaging experience for students.

Āhāra

आहार

One of the finest chemistry laboratories is the human body; taking what is put in it, digesting and synthesizing it to provide energy, vitality, growth, strength, healing and nurturing. We help bring mindfulness to this important aspect of wellness through guidance, counseling, and workshops.

Angela Stanford is an experienced nutrition and wellness expert focused on preventative and integrated health. For over 20 years, she has been promoting mindful eating and lifestyle choices that nurture healthy living while navigating today’s fast-paced, convenience-driven world.

Angela has helped hundreds of people improve their health by choosing what they put on the end of their forks. Angela earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Nutrition from the University of Illinois and completed her Dietetic Internship at UW Hospital and Clinics in Madison, Wisconsin. She holds a Master’s Degree in Business Administration (MBA) from the University of San Francisco. Angela is also credentialed as a Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN) and a Nationally Board-Certified Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC) accredited with the American College of Lifestyle Medicine and the American College of Sports Medicine, and a Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT).

Outside of work, you can find Angela testing out new recipes, balancing on her yoga mat, tending to her organic garden, or hiking trails with friends and family in San Francisco’s East Bay.
